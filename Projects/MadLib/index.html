WILDLIBS

One day Jack was sitting on their favorite heating pad as the sun set for the evening. After a decent nap the cat awoke to the horrifying sound of something downstairs. They rushed toward the sound only to find their Mom vacuuming up a mess. This was very stressful and required some extra time to recover from. If there's one thing about cats it's that they don't always react normally to every situation. After fifteen minutes they finally decided to return to their favorite spot and bathed. After that they remembered that they were very tired and put their head down to nap once more.
